Practice — Cyber Safety Quiz

  1. What makes a password strong?
    • A simple name
    • Short date of birth
    • Mix of letters, numbers & symbols ✔️
  2. Which is a sign of phishing?
    • Misspelled URL ✔️
    • Message from friend with no link
    • Secure website warning
  3. What should you never share online?
    • Your favorite color
    • Your full home address ✔️
    • Your pet’s name

Keep practicing safe online habits!
