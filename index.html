Read — Cyber Safety Basics

Cyber safety refers to protecting personal information, devices, and online accounts from digital threats. Key areas include:

  • Strong, unique passwords
  • Recognizing phishing and scams
  • Safe social media habits
  • Updating software to prevent vulnerabilities
  • Protecting personal data online

Understanding cyber safety helps you stay secure while using the internet for learning, communication, and entertainment.
